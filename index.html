Nampa-tsi Lodge Volunteer Hours Tracker

Track Arrowmen service hours by activity and date.

Embed this tracker

If you saw "Not Found" before, it was likely from using the placeholder URL. Replace your-domain.com with your real site path where these files are hosted.

Option A: iframe embed

<iframe
  src="https://your-domain.com/volunteer-tracker/index.html"
  title="Nampa-tsi Lodge volunteer tracker"
  width="100%"
  height="900"
  style="border:0;"
  loading="lazy"
></iframe>

Option B: direct widget embed (no iframe)

<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://your-domain.com/volunteer-tracker/styles.css" />
<div id="oa-hours-app"></div>
<script src="https://your-domain.com/volunteer-tracker/widget.js"></script>
<script>
  initVolunteerHoursTracker("oa-hours-app");
</script>
